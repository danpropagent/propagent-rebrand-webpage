Trust by default

Output that reviewers can audit.

Every claim has a source. Every source is one click away. Every reviewer sees what the system used.

Source grounding

Every claim is tied back to the source it came from: the RFP, the prior submission, the SME note, the contract precedent. Reviewers see what the system used to write what it wrote.

Claim verification

Statements about the firm's capabilities are checked against accepted, defensible references, not pulled from boilerplate that drifted years ago.

Contradiction detection

When two parts of the response disagree (staffing, schedule, fee posture), the contradiction is surfaced before the submission leaves the firm.

Gap detection

Where the firm is thin or missing support, the gap is shown, not papered over. The team chooses how to position, route an expert question, or hold the claim.

Review visibility

Reviewers can see what the system is doing, what sources were used, and where human judgment is still needed. Trust is observable, not asserted.

What changes for the firm

Four shifts in how the firm pursues work.

Across the workflow and the trust layer, four things change about how the firm qualifies, positions, drafts, and learns from pursuits.

  1. 01

    Pursue better-fit work.

    More informed Go / No-Go decisions. Evaluate fit, competitiveness, requirements, likely effort, and internal readiness before committing time and resources.

    Fewer pursuits chasing the wrong work; more energy on the right work
  2. 02

    Shape stronger responses.

    Understand buyer intent, identify gaps, and shape responses that are more specific to the issuer and the project. Move beyond generic boilerplate.

    Differentiation, not volume of words on the page
  3. 03

    Put existing knowledge to work.

    No months-long static content library to build. Propagent learns from live pursuits, drafts, edits, source documents, prior work, and team decisions as the work happens.

    Usable advantage on pursuit one
  4. 04

    Reduce rework.

    Less time re-chasing the same information, re-assembling the same inputs, and rebuilding the same response logic from scratch. Same quality, less coordination tax.

    Compounds with every pursuit
