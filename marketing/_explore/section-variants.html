From RFP To Response

A11 × A4 · 3 moments

Three biggest-impact moments

Consolidated from five named symptoms into three combined moments — each card carries the weight of 1–2 related pains.

How the five became three:

  • Moment 1 · The vanishing runway = 8-day blind spot + midnight compliance matrix. Both are time / runway issues — RFP found late AND compliance built at the end.
  • Moment 2 · Context drops in the cracks = handoff blackout + PTO-stricken reviewer. Both are information failing to transfer between people (capture→PM, then proposal→reviewer).
  • Moment 3 · The work doesn't sound like the firm = stale past performance + boilerplate-that-doesn't-fit. Both are content quality issues — generic output that reads like every other firm's.

From RFP to response

The three moments that decide a pursuit.

Most pursuit losses trace back to one of these three. Each is a compound of pains we hear in every customer-development call — and each one, on its own, can sink a bid.
